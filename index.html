Road Crossing Assistant

This is my ongoing project in which I aim to build a wearable assistant to aid blind people for crossing road.

New

This is my ongoing project with an to aid blind people for crossing road, more precisely Indian roads where there may not be many traffic signals, and where traffic rules and speed limits may not be followed strictly. For the same, I have collected a dataset of videos. Firstly, I plan to make a machine learning model and use this dataset to predict the safe duration for crossing road in the given video. Then, I plan to integrate this model into a wearable assistant so that blind people can use it for crossing roads in realtime.

Toxic Comment Classification

With the increasing use of social media and open speech platforms, the nuisance of toxic comments is also increasing. The threat of abuse and harassment online means that many people stop expressing themselves and give up on seeking different opinions. As a part of Design Engineering project for my college curriculum, I have made a model for toxic comment classification and integrated it with a Flask web app that allows the user to post comments only if the comment is non-toxic.
